About PodLife
We're PodLife. We created this site so that users would be able to have a much more intuitive and friendly experience with creators. Our goal is to provide you with a way to connect individually with creators and be able to find podcasts which better suit your tastes. Other podcast applications allow for you to follow all sorts of series while ours focuses more on individual creations which allows for greater diversity among our service.
